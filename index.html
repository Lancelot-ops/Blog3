Welcome to Move & Explore — a blog made for those who can't choose between their running shoes and their passport.

Here you will find travel guides for sport lovers, adventure destinations and tips to make the most of every journey. Whether you hike in the mountains, surf on the ocean or run through unknown cities, this blog is made for you.

Surf in Portugal 🏄

Portugal is one of Europe's best surf destinations. From Nazaré's giant waves to Peniche's perfect breaks, the Atlantic coast offers something for every level. Pack your board and get ready for the ride of your life.

Trek in Nepal 🏔️

Nepal is the ultimate destination for hikers. Walking through the Himalayas, surrounded by breathtaking peaks and ancient villages, is an experience that will stay with you forever. Push your limits and discover the roof of the world.

Marathon in Tokyo 🏃

Running through the streets of Tokyo is unlike any other marathon experience. From the neon lights of Shinjuku to the calm of Asakusa, every kilometer brings a new discovery. A race for the body and the soul.
